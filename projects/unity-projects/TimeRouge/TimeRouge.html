Time Rouge

Time Rouge is a fast-paced roguelite shooter where you are relentlessly hunted by time-traveling bounty hunters. Survive against waves of enemies pulled from fractured timelines — from futuristic shock troopers to maddened vikings sent back to slow you down.

Scavenge powerful weapons with unpredictable modifications and acquire game-changing buffs with risky trade-offs. Adapt when you're forced to fight with whatever you can find. Every run is a desperate scramble to stay alive.

When the chase ends, grow stronger. Upgrade your meta progression and prepare for the next attempt — because time is always closing in.
