Gamemaker 2 Raycaster

Being as interested in gaming history as I am it is no suprise that I am endlessly facinated by tech that was developed into products in the early 90's. I have always wanted to implement these concepts to further understand how they work. The ultimate goal would be to implement a rendered like DOOM. However, lets not get ahead of ourselves.

Before ID Software created DOOM, they created Wolfenstein 3D. One of the first first person shooters, and an important stepping stone towards development of the legendary DOOM.

Wolfenstein 3D used a technique called raycasting to render its 3D world. Raycasting is a technique that simulates the way light travels in a scene, allowing for the creation of 3D environments using 2D graphics.

Following a C tutorial by 3DSage (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gYRrGTC7GtA), I have implemented a basic raycaster in Gamemaker 2. Please enjoy adding walls to the map on the left, and pointing at them on the right.
